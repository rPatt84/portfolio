About Me

I am Robert Patterson, i was born in Newcastle Upon Tyne and moved to Nottinghamshire when i was young, This meant i had to learn how to adapt, mainly so i could be understood when communicating, although i have not mastered it yet, i am getting there!

In my free time i enjoy running, sessions in the gym, reading, walking and gaming. Although not religious myself i volunteer at the local church, helping with clock winding, maintenance when required and occasionally interment of ashes.

I enjoy creating user-friendly intuitive designs using HTML, CSS & JavaScript for an optimal user experience.
